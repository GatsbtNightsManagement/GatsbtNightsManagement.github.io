FAQ

Do I have human contact with my manager?

In addition to being able to contact your manager 24/7 via Signal or WhatsApp, regular audit sessions are organized. These enable us to propose a D.A. and ideas for improving your visibility, take stock of what's working and what's not, discuss your development prospects (product placement, advertising, etc.) or simply talk about your well-being, which is at the heart of our agency.

Who owns my contents?

The model retains ownership of her content. The contract authorizes us to promote your content and use it on your behalf for a given period.

How do I get paid?

Payments go directly to your account and we bill you for our services at a fixed period.

How much does it cost?

It costs you NOTHING, we just take a percentage of the revenue we generate for you.

How often should I produce content?

Our models produce content on a weekly basis (5 to 10 hours, or 2 half-days). We also use A.I. content production with private software to reduce our models' content production time. There are also regular photo shoots.
